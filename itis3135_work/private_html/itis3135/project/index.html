CHARLOTTE HEALTH HUB


Our goal is to serve as the central resource for providng and gathering general healthcare info for patients in the Charlotte area,
offering information on Emergency Room (ER) visits, inpatient admissions, outpatient visits, and clinic visits.

and improve their next healthcare visit within the greater Charlotte area. We hope the
information we have gathered will help save you time and money.
