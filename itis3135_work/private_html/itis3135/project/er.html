EMERGENCY ROOM VISITS


The biggest question when it come to emergency room visits is it necessary to be in
the emergency department or could you go to urgent care instead? It is not uncommon
for people with general pain, nausa, or even a fever to hastily visit the emergency room
and thus, pay substatially more at the ER for treatment, when they could get the
same care at an urgent care center.

  • The average cost of an ER visit in Charlotte, NC is about $1600!
  • The average cost for an Urgent Care center in Charlotte, NC is $165.
