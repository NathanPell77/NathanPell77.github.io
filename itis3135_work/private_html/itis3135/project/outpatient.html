OUTPATIENT VISITS


An Outpatient is a patient who receives medical treatment without being admitted to a hospital. Being an outpaitent is
quite literally the opposite of an inpatient because it requires treatment that can be provided without staying overnight.
It is likely that any outpatient at a hospital is there because the were an inpatient. The most common outpatient
visits consist of chest pain, severe allergies, skin issues, back pain, and follow-up exams. Outpatient visits
should last anywhere between as little as 15 minutes to as long as 4-5 hours

  • The average cost for an outpatient visit in Charlotte, NC is $80-$130.
