CLNIC VISITS


You might be asking, arent most hospitals considered a clinic as well? Not exactly. A Clinic is a health care center
where you receive routine preventative care when you are healthy or visit your Doctor/Primary Care Provider when you are ill.
Clinics are almost always smaller than a hospital where patients are less sick and do not stay overnight. Examples
of clinics include things like; where you get yearly physicals, the dentist, the dermatoligist, and a
therapist office. With all these different clinics there are some price differences between them.
Provided below is a list with the average cost per visit for each of the listed clinics

  • The average cost for a pysical in Charlotte, NC is $110.
  • The average cost for a dentist in Charlotte, NC is $100.
  • The average cost for a dermatoligist in Charlotte, NC is $125.
  • The average cost for a therapist in Charlotte, NC is $155.
