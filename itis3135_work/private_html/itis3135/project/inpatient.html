INPATIENT ADMISSIONS


An Inpatient is a person who, in most cases, stays in the hospital overnight and meets a set of clinical criteria.
The clinical critera you have to meet is that you need to be injured/sick enough to stay overnight. This almost
always means constant monitoring, IV's, and treatments you can only recieve at a hospital. The most
common cases are things like chest pain, loss of conciousness, skin infections, and surguries.
An inpatient usually stays at a hospital anywhere from one night to a week.

  • The average cost per night in Charlotte, NC as an inpatient is $2500-$3000 a day
