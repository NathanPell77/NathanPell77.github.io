Who I am

Thank you for visiting my page. Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Nathan Pell and I am a student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I grew up in the city just near SouthPark Mall, and throughout my childhood I played many sports but the one I fell in love with was Soccer. I would love for people that have my same passion to be able to play the game they love with others.

What I do

My goal with this website and hobby group is to gather people of all ages within Charlotte, NC for pickup Soccer games. I serve as a medium to schedule matches and provide equipment for everyone who loves the beautiful game.

So I can register you in my database, please fill out the information below

Where we Play

In order for this to be a successful project, of course we need places to play. I will try to be as fair as possible when it comes to location, hopefully no one has to drive more than 20ish minutes to come have fun.

Places where we can play (open to suggestions)
  • Freedom Park
  • Remblewood Soccer complex
  • Harrisburg Road Sports complex
  • UNCC Recreational fields
  • Any open public highschool field (last resort)

When we Play

Another thing we'll need to make this a successful project are the dates and times of when we will play. In order to accomodate this I will need extra member input as we gather more people to play. The times we play at never have to go full length and you are allowed to leave whenever you want.

Times when we usually play (open to suggestions)
  • Wednesdays, 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Fridays, 6:45 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Saturdays, 2:00 PM - 4:30 PM
  • Sundays, 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM

How we Play

After all of this info you might be asking how we play. In terms of grounds, We will always be playing on a grass/turf field. In terms of equipment, absolutely nothing is required, although I do reccomend shin guards and cleats & I will provide the soccer balls. Lastly our style of play. Our style will always depend on how people are feeling and how many people we have on any given day, it could range from the Champions League finals to casual middle-school recess. In the end the ideal is an equal number of people on either team, and 2 goalies in each goal

Please respond with how you like to play, and what position you like to play

Why We Play

Last but not least, why we play. I believe we play for many reasons. Some play to stay healthy, some play to relax, some even play for a thrill, but most importantly I think we all play because of our love for the game. Soccer isn't called "The beautiful game" for no reason.

Please leave a review of your experience on my website and how I can imrove it.
